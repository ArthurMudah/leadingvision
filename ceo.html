CEO's Message

I welcome you to our page; this is the official website of Leading Vision. I take this opportunity to congratulate all those hugely committed individuals and institutions who out of generosity supported us to the course of orphans, destitute children, HIV/AIDS infected orphans and street children since the inception of Leading Vision. We are so much grateful in whichever way you render that support to us and we wish you well in all that you do. Our mission to support the Girl Child and the malnourished children remains unwavering. Management has added two programs which is the Girl child Education, Health and Nutrition for malnourish children. The introduction of the programs are very significant and achievement for Leading Vision. The Girl Child education and the Nutrition program will try to end some cultural myth, Religious beliefs and traditions that marginalize girls, whiles the nutrition initiative will also tackle the assertion that malnourished children are cursed. We say no to those assertions and beliefs. Leading Vision will bridge the gap between our target groups and the solution to challenges. As Leading Vision yearns to become one of the leading charity organizations, this can only be achieved by our call for global partnership to fund our projects and programs which you can find on this website. It is my ardent desire that where we could not reach we will link the upcoming generation to reach and see far than what we have seen. The girl child cannot be denied education whiles we keep quiet, we affirm to stand by them to advocate and support them so they acquire education, and in so doing we are giving them a future. Giving to the cause of the less privileged can help us take orphans, destitute children from the street and become prominent in society. As we begin this year, it is my prayer that we join together and with your donation we can execute our planned projects and programs. You can link or introduce us to a friend or groups, organizations or institutions and philanthropist so we can achieve our goal of being closer to the needy. Leading Vision remains accountable for any donation to its program. You can donate to us by using our donation form on our page. You can direct all your questions, suggestions or support to us by using our contact page.

Thanks for your attention.
